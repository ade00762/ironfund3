About Us

Iron Fund Management Limited is a premier investment management firm that provides professional fund management services to individuals, institutions, and corporations

We offer tailored investment solutions designed to maximize returns while managing risk across a range of asset classes, including equities, bonds, real estate, and money markets

At Iron Fund, we partner with our clients to grow and preserve their wealth across various asset classes with consideration for future generations.

Our Mission

To build the most trusted and preferred investment bank in Nigeria.

Services

Iron Fund collaborates closely with you to understand your unique requirements and deliver tailored solutions that meet your objectives across our full range of services.

Investment Portfolio Management

We manage investment portfolios for individuals, institutions, and corporate clients, focusing on maximizing returns while minimizing risks.

Wealth Management

We provide personalized financial planning and investment strategies for high-net-worth individuals, aimed at preserving and growing wealth over the long term.

Equity & Fixed Income

We manage investments in both the equity markets (stocks) and fixed-income securities (bonds) to achieve balanced risk-adjusted returns.

Asset Allocation and Diversification

We offer advice on asset allocation across various financial products like stocks, bonds, and real estate to meet the client's financial objectives..

Alternative Investments

We Provide access to alternative asset classes such as private equity, hedge funds, real estate, venture capital, and commodities.

Risk Management and Diversification

We implement strategies to manage risk, including diversifying investments across sectors and markets to reduce exposure to any single asset class.

Market Research and Analysis

We offer in-depth market research and financial analysis to inform investment decisions, helping clients capitalize on opportunities and avoid potential risks

Liquidity and Cash Management

We manage short-term investments and ensuring liquidity for clients, particularly institutions, to meet operational or emergency needs without sacrificing investment returns.
